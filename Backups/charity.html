Charity

Journey will partner with Walking Shield, a nonprofit organization whose mission is to improve the quality of life for American Indian families. Our social responsibility will play a big role in our company, as we will constantly be donating backpacks filled with goods to the children of different Native American tribes. In these packs, we will include basic necessities such as: clothes, school supplies, hygiene products, and activities. Through this social involvement, we hope to secure promising futures for children who are not as fortunate. By partnering with Walking Shield, we help orchestrate a plan for the improvement of education on Native American reservations. As well as sending supplies to the children of Native American tribes, each month, one of our personality packs will be a designated charity pack. 50% of the proceeds from the chosen pack will be sent to Walking Shield.
