Our Products

We have a set of products that appeal to anyone seeking to develop their identities through creative expression. There are 6 different packs to explore. Each pack can be purchased in small, medium, and large sizes, named Trek, Excursion, and Expedition, respectively. Trek contains one outdoor activity, one indoor activity, new music to explore and a single miscellaneous item. Excursion doubles the items received, and Expedition contains four of each item.
